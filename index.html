I graduated from high school at the pre-university institute of exact sciences in my province, Villa Clara, Cuba. I studied at this school between 1994 and 1997. This school is oriented to the exact sciences and prepares you to enter university.

Central University of Las Villas, Cuba.

I obtained a bachelor's degree at the Central University of Las Villas, Cuba. I studied at the university between 1998 and 2003. In these five years I studied many subjects related to mathematics and computing. Some of these subjects were:

  • Logic
  • Mathematical analysis
  • Statistics and Probabilities
  • Programming
  • Databases

In my career I learned the Delphi programming language. It is practically obsolete but has a lot in common with C-sharp and C ++. I also studied an introduction to Java and web programming with html and asp.
